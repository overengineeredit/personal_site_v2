## Level Of Effort – T-shirt Sizing Guide

| T-shirt Size | Rough Estimate Range for a squad (2–3 FTEs) |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------- |
| XS           | < 1 Week                                    |
| S            | 1 to 2 weeks (< 1 Sprint)                   |
| M            | 1 – 3 Sprints (< 1.5 Months)                |
| L            | 1.5 – 3 Months (< 1 Quarter)                |
| XL           | > 1 Quarter                                 |
